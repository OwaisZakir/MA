Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our solar installations and services.

Solar energy is renewable, reduces electricity bills, has low maintenance costs, and is environmentally friendly.

Savings depend on various factors including the size of the system, energy consumption, and local electricity rates. Typically, solar panels can reduce your electricity bill by 50-75%.

Solar panels require minimal maintenance. It's recommended to clean them periodically to remove dust and debris and ensure optimal performance.

Solar panels typically last between 25-30 years. Their efficiency gradually decreases over time.

While DIY solar panel kits are available, it's recommended to have a professional installation to ensure safety and efficiency.

Solar panels convert sunlight into electricity using photovoltaic (PV) cells. The electricity can be used immediately, stored in batteries, or fed back into the grid.
